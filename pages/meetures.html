Sometime in November of 2021 I was working on a high school 3d modeling assignment and finished a bit early. Instead of going on my phone I made the conscious decision to make something fun in the 3d modeling program (fusion360). I specifically remember resisting the urge to go on my phone. I spent some time and made this:

Well not exactly this but something similar. Then I decided to make a list of 10 different colors, eyes, feet, and body shapes. I thought it would be cool to make 10,000 of these guys and I even made a script to generate all of them. This is number 00001.
Then I had an idea to make them all into NFTs. I know I know ... this was a terrible idea. I made a Twitter and started writing the script to upload all of them. I even made a Roadmap! Then a video called Line Goes Up came out. I knew some people hated NFT's but I thought they just didn't understand it. But after I watched the video, I understood why people actually hated NFTs. It wasn't that they were all guaranteed scams, it was that *almost* all of them were guaranteed scams. I was essentially wearing a burglar's mask and walking into a bank because I just thought it was fashionable.

So this was a waste of time! Well not entirely. I learned a lot through making this project even if I never ended up posting it on OpenSea. But I was really close to doing so lol. You can look at them if you want though:
